Flooring Types

Hardwood

Hardwood is a classic, elegant, and eye catching flooring for any home or business. There’s a wide variety of colour, texture, shades and grain options to make every floor a unique, luxurious addition to any area. A well maintained hardwood floor can last more than a lifetime, always providing elegance and beauty.

Tile

A safe bet for any entryway, kitchen, bathroom, or anywhere you may get the floor wet. Tile, whether it’s porcelain or ceramic, offers durability and is tough and resistant to moisture. Tile doesn’t just belong on the floor! We offer tiles for your shower, or backsplash too! Just ask any of the MH Floors experts for your tile needs.

Carpet

Carpet offers warmth and comfort in a home or business. And with new stain resistant carpets, your worry of scrubbing stains is over. Carpet is now more durable than ever, and comes in many options from varying plushness, to colour, pattern, and almost anything you can dream of!


Laminate

Laminate flooring provides the same elegant and luxurious look and feel of hardwood, but comes at a more affordable price. There are numerous options available from multi-width laminate, to grey tones, light oaks, dark tones… just about any style and colour you can think of. Plus, it’s a quick and easy install! Talk to any of our MH Floors experts for laminate flooring options!

Other

Your dream floors are almost reality. All you need is the team at MH Floors! We offer more flooring styles than you imagined. From cork, to vinyl, and even incredibly durable industrial flooring, we can help you get the flooring of your dreams.
MH Floors does more than floors! Stairways, showers, and backsplashes are just a few more things we do. The team at MH Floors is ready to help you customize the needs of your project!
